How a Doctor/Staff Member Logs In:

  1. Navigate to the MedNexus Portal.
  2. Click the User Login button on the top right or scroll to the access form.
  3. In the login form:
    • Select your role Doctor/Physician, Nurse/Staff, or Administrator.
    • Enter your unique User ID or Hospital Email Address.
    • Enter your secure Password.
  4. Click Secure Sign In to access your dedicated dashboard .
Go to Login Page

How a Patient Logs In:

  1. Navigate to the MedNexus Patient Portal (usually a sub-link on the main hospital website).
  2. The system will direct you to the Patient Login form.
  3. You will be asked to verify your identity using one of two methods:
    • Method 1 Enter your verified Email Address and a unique Temporary Password provided during your first visit registration.
    • Method 2: Enter your Patient ID and Date of Birth for verification.
  4. Upon successful login, you access your personal health record.

Note: Patient login is typically managed via a separate, secure subdomain for compliance and privacy reasons.
