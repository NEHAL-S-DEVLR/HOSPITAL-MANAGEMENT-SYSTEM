Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a new appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by logging into the Patient Portal, navigating to the **Schedule** section, and selecting **+ New Appointment**. Follow the steps to choose your doctor, service type, and preferred time slot. Confirmation will be sent via email.

Where can I view and pay my hospital bill?

All your billing statements are available under the **Billing & Payments** tab in the MedNexus portal. You can view itemized statements, track insurance claims, and make secure payments using a credit card or bank transfer directly on that page.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page. You will be prompted to enter your registered email address. A password reset link will be sent to your inbox. Be sure to check your spam folder if you don't receive it immediately.
